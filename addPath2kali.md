## `Lets Say I Have To Add Solc Path`
```cmd
export PATH=$PATH: /home/whoami/.local/bin/solc
```

## `Helper Commands`
- `which solc`
  ```cmd.sh
  /home/whoami/.local/bin/solc   #path of solc 
  ```
